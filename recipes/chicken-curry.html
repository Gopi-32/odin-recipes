Chicken Curry

Description

This aromatic chicken curry is bursting with flavors and spices. It's a warming dish that combines tender chicken with a rich, savory sauce, perfect for serving over rice or with naan bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken pieces
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Saut√© the onions until they are soft and translucent.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, and spices (curry powder, turmeric, cumin) and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and brown them on all sides.
  4. Mix in the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and let the mixture simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce thickens.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve hot with rice or naan.

Chocolate Cake

Description

This rich and moist chocolate cake is perfect for celebrations and everyday indulgence. With a decadent flavor and a soft, fluffy texture, it's a treat for everyone.

Enjoy a slice with a cup of coffee or share it with friends and family at your next gathering.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract; beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in the boiling water (the batter will be thin), then pour it evenly into the pans.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.

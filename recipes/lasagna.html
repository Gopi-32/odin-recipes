Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food made with layers of tender pasta, hearty meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese. This dish is perfect for a family dinner or a special occasion.

The blend of savory flavors and textures makes every bite satisfying. Whether you're serving it on a busy weeknight or for a weekend gathering, lasagna is always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, sauté the diced onion and minced garlic until softened.
  4. Add the ground beef and cook until it is browned, seasoning with salt and pepper.
  5. Stir in the marinara sauce and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce, then layer with noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Allow the lasagna to cool for 10 minutes before serving.
